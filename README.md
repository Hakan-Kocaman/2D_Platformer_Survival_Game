# Pygame-Project-Video-Game-
1. The Core Objective: "Survive the Waves"
The game is built around Waves. It starts relatively easy, but as you progress, the undead become faster, tougher, and more numerous. Your goal is simple: survive as many waves as possible and beat your "Personal Best."

2. The Gameplay Loop
Fight: You move across the screen, jumping over obstacles and shooting down enemies coming from both sides.

Loot: When enemies die, they drop Skulls. These aren't just trophies—they are the game's currency.

Upgrade: Between (or during) the chaos, you head to the Shop. You can spend your collected skulls to permanently upgrade your Cowboy's stats, such as:

Maximum Health: So you can take more hits.

Bullet Damage: To drop enemies faster.

Fire Rate: To turn your revolvers into lead-spitting machines.

3. Your Secret Weapons
To survive the later waves, you have two unique mechanics:

Deadeye Mode: Ever wanted to move like a gunslinger in a movie? Activating Deadeye slows down time for everyone but you. It consumes energy but allows you to dodge projectiles and line up perfect shots while the zombies move in slow motion.

Barrel Fortifications: When you take down "Tank" zombies, you earn Barrels. You can place these on the ground to create instant barricades. The zombies will have to chew through the wood before they can get to you, giving you precious time to reload.

4. The Rogue's Gallery (The Enemies)
You aren't just fighting mindless shufflers. You’ll encounter:

Basic Zombies: Slow but dangerous in groups.

Tanks: Massive, high-health brutes that require a lot of lead to bring down.

Souls: Ghostly, flying enemies. If they touch you, you become "Haunted," which temporarily slows your movement and jams your trigger finger.

5. Controls at a Glance
The game uses a classic, intuitive setup:

Move & Jump: A, D, and W (or Arrow Keys).

Shoot: Spacebar.

Deadeye: R (Slow down time).

Build: E (Drop a defensive barrel).

Pro Tip: Don't let the Souls touch you! Being "Haunted" while a Tank is charging is the fastest way to see the "Game Over" screen.

6. Player Rising
Between the chaos, the player interacts with a menu that acts as a Stat Modifier Terminal. By spending collected skulls, you are essentially overwriting the base variables of the Cowboy:

Durability Scaling: Increasing the "Max Health" variable allows the player to survive more collision events.

Throughput Scaling: By decreasing the "Fire Rate" delay (the cooldown between shots), the player increases their DPS (Damage Per Second) without necessarily changing the damage per bullet.

Output Scaling: Directly increasing the "Damage" variable reduces the number of hits required to trigger an enemy's "Death" state.
